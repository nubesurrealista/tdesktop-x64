## An open letter about a unified effort

This fork would not be possible without the incredible work done at [64gram](https://github.com/TDesktop-x64/tdesktop) and the admirable work done on that frknkrc44 [fork]( https://github.com/frknkrc44/tdesktop-x64). My fork is intended for my personal use, but I am open to collaboration (within my limits) and receiving help. Several people have forks of 64gram, but we are not united in a unified effort. From here, I want to say that if you want to improve your fork, my fork, and the ecosystem for everyone, we must focus our efforts on contributing among our forks and having a common base that benefits us all.
